Privacy Policy

LK OBDII Scanner for Volvo

Last updated: July 2026

Thank you for using LK OBDII Scanner for Volvo. Your privacy is important. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles your information.

Information Collection

LK OBDII Scanner for Volvo does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The application does not require user registration and does not create user accounts.

Bluetooth Permission

The application uses Bluetooth only to communicate with a compatible ELM327 OBD-II adapter connected to your vehicle.

Bluetooth is never used for tracking users or collecting personal information.

Vehicle Data

Vehicle diagnostic information, live parameters, fault codes (DTC), and other vehicle data are processed only on your device.

No vehicle information is transmitted to external servers.

Internet Access

The current version of the application does not upload any vehicle data or personal information to the Internet.

Third-Party Services

The current version of the application does not use:

  • Advertising services
  • Analytics services
  • Cloud storage
  • User tracking services

Future versions may include optional advertising or analytics. If this happens, this Privacy Policy will be updated before those features are released.

Permissions

Depending on the Android version, the application may request permissions required for:

  • Bluetooth communication
  • Nearby devices
  • Location (required by Android for Bluetooth scanning on some versions)

These permissions are used exclusively to connect to compatible Bluetooth OBD-II adapters.

Children's Privacy

The application is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any future changes will be published on this page.
